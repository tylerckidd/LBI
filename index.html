About LBI

LBI helps guide students to their future careers.It provides the essentials that can help them gain inspiration for what careers they may want in the future.
Most of the students I knew while working in a school setting for over 5 years wanted to be a basketball player or a football player but did not give me many options other than that.
I wanted to provide students with a educational app that introduces them to skills that are needed on the job market in today's economy.
Most students do not know what they want to go to college to be and hopefully with this app it can spark ideas early on.
